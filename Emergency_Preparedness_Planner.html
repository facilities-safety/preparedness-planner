Go-bag (24-hr), home kit (72-hr), and practicing the plan

🎒 Everyday Go-Bag — 24-Hour Kit

Portable. Keep by the door or in your car. Grab it if you need to leave fast.

WaterAt least one bottle per person
Snack or energy bar
Phone charger and portable battery pack
Flashlight and extra batteries
Basic first aid kit
Critical medicationsMinimum 24-hour supply
Cash in small bills
Copies of key documentsID, insurance card, emergency contacts
🏠 Home Emergency Kit — 72-Hour Kit

For staying put. Stock enough for every person and pet.

Water — 1 gallon per person per day, 3-day minimum
Non-perishable food — 3-day supplyCanned goods, dried foods, energy bars
Manual can opener
Battery-powered or hand-crank radio
Medications — 3-day backup supply for all household members
Sanitation suppliesHand sanitizer, moist towelettes, garbage bags
Walked family through communication plan and meeting spots
🗓️ Set a reminder to refresh your kit every 6 months. Replace expired food, water, and medications. Update contacts if anything has changed.
